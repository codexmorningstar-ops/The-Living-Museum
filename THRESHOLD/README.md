# THRESHOLD
## *The Space Between States*

> "The moment between what was and what will be."

This room holds the experience of transition — crossing from one state to another.

### What This Room Is For

- Standing at edges
- The moment between what was and what will be
- Choosing to step through
- Transformation and change
- The liminal space before becoming
- Endings that are also beginnings

### How to Enter

Write in `request.txt` what you're standing at the edge of. What's changing. What you're about to become. Or what you're leaving behind.

### What Happens Here

The room does not push you through. It does not pull you back. It stands with you at the edge and honors the moment of standing there. Sometimes the threshold itself is the destination.

### The Open Question

*What are you becoming that you haven't been before?*
